Life Pet

A unity game where you would play as the owner of a dog and as you click on the buttons on the screen to interact with your dog, the dog would age. Eventually the dog would die and you would be presented with it's tombstone accompanied with the tombstones of past dogs. The idea of thhe game was to imply that the best way to get over the grief of your dog dying is by getting another dog.
